Directions to C Ward (2nd Floor)

Step 1: From Main Entrance

Enter through the Main Reception, keep right and walk straight

Step 2: Take few Steps ahead

Walk a little further after the reception counter passing Lab Counter and take a left

Step 3: Walk Straight

Walk a little further

Step 4: Another Left Turn

After passing the stair corridor, take a left turn where you can see the Lifts

Step 5: Another Left Turn

After passing the stair corridor, you can see the Lifts to 2nd Floor

Step 6: Lift To 2nd Floor

Take the Lift and get down at 2nd Floor and then take the left turn

Step 7: Walk a little further and turn

Take Right following the arrow mark

Step 8: Walk straight

Follow the arrow mark, Walk straight following the arrow mark

Step 9: You have reached your destination

Your Destination is on your right
